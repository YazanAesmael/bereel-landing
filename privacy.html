01 — Who we are

BeReel is a short-form video platform for real moments.

BeReel ("we", "us", "our") operates the BeReel app and bereel.app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use BeReel, how we use it, and the choices you have.

If anything here is unclear, write to us at admin@bereel.app. We'll answer.

02 — What we collect

Only what we need to make BeReel work.

Information you give us

  • Account information — your email address, username, display name, and (optionally) a bio and profile photo.
  • Videos — every video you record in the app, including its audio. Your videos are public to other BeReel users by default.
  • Captions, comments, likes, follows, and reports — the things you write and the actions you take in the app.
  • Waitlist email — if you join the waitlist on our website, we store your email address so we can let you in when the app is ready.
  • Communications — messages you send us at admin@bereel.app, including any information you choose to include.

Information we collect automatically

  • Approximate location — when you record a video, we may attach a general location (such as a city) if you've granted location permission. You can turn this off at any time in your device settings.
  • Watch behavior — which videos you watch and roughly how long you watch them. We use this to keep the feed feeling fresh and to push down low-quality content.
  • Device and app information — basic technical information such as your device type, operating system version, and the BeReel app version, so we can fix bugs and ship updates that work on your device.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect data about you from other apps or websites.
  • We do not run third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not buy data about you from data brokers.
03 — How we use it

To run the app. Nothing else.

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To show your videos and profile to other users.
  • To rank and serve the feed.
  • To enforce our Terms and Community Guidelines and to review reports.
  • To respond when you contact us.
  • To send you essential service emails (account, security, important updates). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To diagnose problems and improve the app.
  • To comply with the law if we are legally required to.
04 — What we don't do

The short list of nevers.

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your personal information with advertisers.
  • We do not run third-party advertising on BeReel.
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps.
  • We do not profile you by race, ethnicity, religion, politics, sexuality, or anything else of that nature.

BeReel exists because the rest of the internet does these things by default. We do not, and we will not.

05 — Who else sees it

The smallest circle we can manage.

Other BeReel users

Other users see your username, display name, profile photo, bio, follower count, and the videos you publish. If you do not want something to be public, do not publish it.

Service providers

BeReel relies on a small set of trusted third-party providers to host the app, deliver email, and keep things running. These providers process information on our behalf, only for the purposes we tell them to, and are bound by their own privacy obligations.

Law enforcement and legal requests

We disclose information when we are legally required to — for example, in response to a valid legal order. Where the law allows, we will tell you before we do.

If BeReel changes hands

If BeReel is acquired, merged, or restructured, your information may transfer to the new entity. If that happens, we will tell you, and the new entity will be bound by this policy or one at least as protective.

06 — Your choices

You are in control.

  • Edit your profile. Your display name, bio, and profile photo can be changed in the app at any time.
  • Delete your videos. You can delete any video you've posted. It comes off the feed immediately.
  • Turn off location. Use your device's settings to revoke location permission for BeReel at any time.
  • Delete your account. Open the BeReel app, go to Settings, and tap Delete Account. Your account and content are removed from our active systems. If you can't access the app, write to admin@bereel.app from the address on the account and we will do it for you.
  • Leave the waitlist. Email admin@bereel.app and we will remove your address.

Depending on where you live, you may also have the right to access, correct, port, or object to certain processing of your information. To exercise any of these rights, write to admin@bereel.app. We will respond.

07 — How long we keep it

As briefly as we reasonably can.

  • Account information — for as long as your account exists. Deleted when you delete your account.
  • Videos — until you delete them or your account.
  • Reports and moderation records — kept longer than other data, because they are how we keep the platform safe.
  • Waitlist email — until you ask us to remove it, or until BeReel launches and the waitlist is closed.
  • Backups — deleted information may persist briefly in routine backups before it is overwritten.
08 — Security

We take it seriously. We are also honest about the limits.

We use industry-standard practices to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest where it is available. Access to user data inside BeReel is limited to people who need it to do their job.

That said: no system on the internet is 100% secure. If we ever discover a breach that affects your information, we will tell you as quickly as we can.

09 — Children

BeReel is for users 16 and over.

BeReel is not intended for anyone under 16, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has given us information, write to admin@bereel.app and we will delete it.

10 — Changes

We will tell you when this changes.

If we update this policy in a way that materially affects you, we will change the effective date at the top of this page and notify you in the app, by email, or both. Continued use of BeReel after a change means you accept the updated policy.

11 — Contact

One inbox.

For any privacy question, request, or complaint, write to admin@bereel.app. A real person will read it.
